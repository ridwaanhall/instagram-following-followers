How to Get Your Instagram Data

Follow these step-by-step instructions to download your Instagram following and followers data

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Go to your Instagram profile and tap the menu icon.

2

Click the top-right icon with three horizontal lines.

3

Click "Account Center".

4

In account settings, select "Your Information and Permissions".

5

Click "Download Your Information".

6

Click "Download or Transfer Information".

7

Select your account.

8

Select "Some of your information".

9

In the "Connections" section, select "Followers and Following".

10

Download to your device.

11

Set the date range to "All time" and format to "JSON".

12

Click the "Create Files" button.

✓

After a few minutes, navigate to "Download Your Information" to get your files.

🎉 You're almost done! Instagram will prepare your data.
