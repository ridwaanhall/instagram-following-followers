Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Instagram follower analysis, data privacy, and getting the most out of InstaAnalytics

? How do I get my Instagram followers and following data?

Download your Instagram data by going to Settings → Privacy → Download Your Information. Select "Followers and Following" from the connections section and choose JSON format. Our step-by-step tutorial provides detailed instructions with screenshots for both mobile and desktop.

Is my Instagram data safe and private?

Absolutely! Your Instagram data is processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No data is ever uploaded to our servers, stored in databases, or shared with third parties. We prioritize your privacy and security above everything else.

What specific insights and analytics will I get?

You'll get comprehensive analytics including: non-followers list (people you follow who don't follow back), mutual connections with follow dates, recent activity tracking (last 30 days), engagement percentages, follower-to-following ratios, clickable Instagram profile links, and timestamp-based sorting for all lists.

Do I need to log in or provide my Instagram credentials?

No login required! Simply upload or paste your Instagram data export files. We never ask for your Instagram username, password, or any login credentials. The tool works completely offline with your exported data files.

What file formats and input methods are supported?

We support three convenient methods: (1) JSON file upload - followers_1.json and following.json files, (2) ZIP file upload - complete Instagram data export with automatic extraction, and (3) Direct text input - copy and paste JSON data directly into text areas.

How can this help me grow my Instagram account?

Use the insights to clean up your following list by unfollowing non-followers, identify genuine engagement through mutual connections, track growth patterns with timestamp data, and optimize your follow strategy based on follow-back rates and engagement metrics.
